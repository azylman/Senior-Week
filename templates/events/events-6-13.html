WEDNESDAY, JUNE 13
VOLUNTEER AT RONALD MCDONALD HOUSE
bus leaves at 10:00AM - no cost
This year, we are proud to offer community service opportunities for the first time as part of Senior Week! We will be taking small groups of NU seniors to the Chicago Ronald McDonald House for Children's Memorial Hospital to cook lunch for the children and their families.
CHICAGO ARCHITECTURE BOAT TOUR
12:00PM - 400 N. Michigan Avenue - $15.60
This is the quintessential Chicago experience. This 90-minute guided river cruise provides an overview of the historic and modern architectural styles of Chicago. The June weather will be perfect and the boat's onboard beer bar certainly doest hurt! Transportation to this event is up to you, but we recommend taking the El downtown and grabbing lunch before we board! Spots are limited.
